One-way Audio Transmitter

Fundamentals of Electronic Circuits PCB CAD

A compact one-way audio transmitter built as the final project for Fundamentals of Electronic Circuits. The design uses a discrete RF front-end with tuned LC filtering, a two-stage amplifier chain, and an audio output stage. The submission includes prototype photos and the full schematic/PCB artwork.

Video demonstration of the one-way audio transmitter in action, showcasing its audio transmission capabilities and performance in a real-world environment.

24-Shot Clock

Logic Circuit and Design CAD PCB

A 24-shot timing system implemented for the Logic Circuit and Design course. The project uses synchronous counters, decoding logic, and timed outputs to sequence 24 events. The CAD exploded views show the mechanical arrangement and PCB stacks; a short demo video accompanies the CAD images to illustrate the sequence in action.

Video demonstration of the 24-shot clock in operation, showing the timing sequence and output behavior as it progresses through all 24 shots.
